How to review

Review the experience, not just the screens.

Each walkthrough asks you to move through the app like a family would: understand the situation, find the next action, judge whether the supporting evidence is trustworthy, and notice where confidence breaks down.

01 Situation

Can you tell which athlete, stage, school, or follow-up problem you are solving?

02 Flow

Does the app guide you from overview to the right workspace without hunting?

03 Trust

Can you distinguish sourced information, estimates, gaps, and user-entered notes?

04 Next action

After the scenario, is the next task or decision obvious enough to act on?

05 Fallback path

If you cannot navigate intuitively, use the click-by-click steps in the walkthrough and tell us where you needed them.

Private alpha only

Synthetic people

Demo players, contacts, outreach notes, and media records are synthetic and not affiliated with schools.

Local-first records

The app stores athlete planning records locally unless a user exports a backup or opens an external link.
