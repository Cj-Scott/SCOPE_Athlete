Workflow B / School shortlist

Can reviewers trust the school recommendation path?

Use this script to test whether fit, source confidence, evidence, and next actions are understandable.

25 minutes Pipeline + Intelligence Evidence and gaps

Walkthrough script

Review one shortlist decision.

  1. 01
    Load demo data.

    Start with the same demo records so feedback is comparable.

  2. 02
    Open School Pipeline.

    Look at the top three recommendations and whether the cards are easy to scan.

  3. 03
    Open one school detail view.

    Use the recommended action or detail button for one school that catches your eye.

  4. 04
    Review academics and soccer fit.

    Check whether fit score, academics, program notes, and position pathway feel connected.

  5. 05
    Review source confidence.

    Decide whether evidence, missing fields, and source labels make the recommendation trustworthy.

  6. 06
    Try sorting or filtering.

    Confirm the pipeline responds in a predictable way and preserves the comparison context.

Quick check

  • Fit clarity

    Do the school fit reasons make sense?

  • Evidence clarity

    Can you tell what is sourced, missing, or estimated?

  • Action clarity

    Do you know what to do after reviewing the school?

Feedback prompts

Answer these after the walkthrough.

  • Did the recommendation feel explainable?

    Note the specific score, label, or evidence that helped or hurt trust.

  • Were gaps obvious?

    Call out missing data, unclear source confidence, or school fields that need manual support.

  • Could you compare options quickly?

    Describe whether the top-three recommendation limit helped or hid useful context.

  • What would make school review safer?

    Suggest wording, warnings, or source details that would prevent over-trusting weak data.
