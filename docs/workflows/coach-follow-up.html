Workflow C / Coach follow-up

Can outreach, video, and tasks feel connected?

Use this script to test whether a family can trace a coach interaction into video proof, task ownership, and a next follow-up.

20 minutes Scenario: active follow-up Outreach + Video + Tasks

Scenario

A family needs to follow up with a coach.

They have a school-linked contact note, a public video reference, and a next action that should be easy to find without storing private email content.

Experience goal

The app should connect notes, proof, and action.

Reviewers should understand who the contact is, what happened, which video or task matters, and what the family should do next.

Feature touchpoints

  • Outreach list and contact detail
  • School-linked notes
  • Video link status and public accessibility
  • Task status, owner, and due date
  • Pipeline context for the related school

Walkthrough script

Trace one follow-up thread.

  1. 01
    Load demo data and choose an active outreach player.

    Use the same synthetic player and school records as other reviewers.

  2. 02
    Open Outreach.

    Confirm it reads as contact notes tied to a school, not as email storage.

  3. 03
    Review one contact thread.

    Check contact name, contact title, date, response status, notes, next follow-up, and school relationship.

  4. 04
    Open the related school or pipeline context.

    Decide whether the school status and interest level explain why this follow-up matters.

  5. 05
    Open Video.

    Review one video link and decide whether public accessibility status is understandable.

  6. 06
    Open Tasks.

    Find the follow-up task connected to outreach or video context.

  7. 07
    Create or inspect a next action.

    Judge whether status labels, due dates, task ownership, and task source are clear enough.

Quick check

  • Outreach meaning

    Is the section clearly a notes tracker?

  • Video status

    Can you tell whether a link is accessible?

  • Next action

    Is the follow-up task obvious?

  • Story continuity

    Can you explain the follow-up thread after leaving the app?

Feedback prompts

Answer these after the walkthrough.

  • Was Outreach named and shaped correctly?

    Note whether contact title, contact name, date, school, and notes were enough.

  • Was video validation clear?

    Call out any link status, source, or warning that felt confusing.

  • Did tasks connect to the follow-up story?

    Describe whether the task made sense after reviewing outreach and video.

  • Did the full scenario hold together?

    Call out any point where school, contact, video, and task context felt disconnected.

  • What redundant button or label got in the way?

    Identify any action that felt unlikely to be used during follow-up.
