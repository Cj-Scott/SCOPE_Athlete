Private alpha FAQ

A few practical answers before you review.

This page covers the basics: what to test, what not to enter, how the Windows warning works, how demo data works, and where feedback goes.

Quick answers

Start here if something feels unclear.

Keep the review simple. Use the synthetic demo data, follow one workflow, and tell us where the experience helps or breaks down.

What is SCOPE Athlete?

SCOPE Athlete is a planning workspace for student-athlete families. SCOPE stands for School Comparison & Opportunity Planning Engine.

What should I test?

Pick one walkthrough: parent onboarding, school shortlist review, or coach follow-up. The goal is to judge the workflow, not every button.

Should I enter real information?

No. Please use synthetic demo data only. Do not enter real athlete, family, school-contact, or private planning information during alpha review.

Why are downloads paused?

Installer downloads are paused until August 10, 2026 while the next review window is staged. Videos, walkthroughs, and feedback remain available.

Why does Windows warn during install?

The alpha installer is not production code-signed yet. When downloads reopen, continue only if the file came from the official SCOPE Athlete page.

What are checksums for?

Checksums help verify that a downloaded installer matches the file that was published. They are a safety check for reviewers who are comfortable using them.

How do I load demo data?

When the app is available, open Settings and use the demo data/reset controls. The demo data is synthetic and exists so you can review without private records.

How do I reset the alpha experience?

Use the alpha reset controls in Settings. If you created any local records you want to keep, export a backup first.

How do I uninstall it?

Use Windows Settings, then Apps, then Installed apps, then SCOPE Athlete, then Uninstall.

Where do updates come from?

The app separates signed app updates from lighter data-pack updates for school, rules, and evidence data. Review update prompts before applying anything.

Where does feedback go?

Feedback goes through the linked Google Form. Responses are reviewed manually before anything becomes product work.

What feedback is most useful?

Tell us what page you were on, what you expected, what happened, and whether it made the workflow feel more or less clear, useful, or trustworthy.
